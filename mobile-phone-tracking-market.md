<p><p>The mobile phone tracking market is divided into two main types: indoor and outdoor tracking. Indoor tracking focuses on locating mobile devices within enclosed spaces, using technologies like Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, and RFID to enhance accuracy in complex environments like malls or airports. Outdoor tracking, on the other hand, utilizes GPS, cellular networks, and satellite systems to determine device locations over larger distances, ideal for navigation and route optimization in urban and rural areas. Both types cater to security, logistics, and personal tracking needs.</p></p>
