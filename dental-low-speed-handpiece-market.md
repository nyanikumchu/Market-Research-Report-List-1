<p><p>The dental low-speed handpiece market includes two main types: straight handpieces and bend handpieces. Straight handpieces feature a straight design, providing clinicians with direct access to various areas in the mouth, making them ideal for procedures requiring precision. In contrast, bend handpieces, with an angled neck, are designed to reach difficult-to-access areas, enhancing maneuverability during restorative and surgical procedures. Both types are essential tools in dentistry, contributing to efficiency and effectiveness in patient care.</p></p>
