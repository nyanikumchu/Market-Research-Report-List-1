<p><strong>The Medical Vacuum Filters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Deep Filter</li><li>Cellulose Membrane Filter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical vacuum filters market encompasses various types, including deep filters and cellulose membrane filters. Deep filters capture particles through a thick, porous medium, effectively trapping larger contaminants while allowing fluid flow. Cellulose membrane filters, on the other hand, utilize a thin, fine membrane, typically made from cellulose, to filter out microorganisms and particulate matter. Both types are crucial for ensuring sterile environments and are widely used in laboratory, pharmaceutical, and medical device applications to maintain cleanliness and safety.</p></p>
