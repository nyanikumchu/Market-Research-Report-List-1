<p><p>The Cold Cathode Ionization Gauge Tube market is primarily segmented into Magnetron and Inverted Magnetron types. Magnetron gauges utilize a magnetic field to control electron paths, enhancing ion production for pressure measurement, making them suitable for high vacuum applications. Inverted Magnetron gauges, on the other hand, offer improved sensitivity and stability, employing an inverted design that reduces sputtering of the electrode, ideal for ultra-high vacuum environments. Both types are crucial in various industrial and research applications.</p></p>
