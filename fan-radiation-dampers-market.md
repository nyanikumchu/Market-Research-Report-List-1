<p><p>The Fan Radiation Dampers Market is categorized into two main types: Single Blade Type and Dual Blade Type. Single Blade Dampers consist of a single movable blade that adjusts airflow management in response to temperature changes, providing basic radiation control. In contrast, Dual Blade Dampers feature two blades working in tandem, offering enhanced performance and tighter sealing against airflow, improving efficiency in preventing heat transfer. Both types play a crucial role in HVAC systems, ensuring compliance with fire and safety regulations.</p></p>
