<p><ul><li>Amplifying Stethoscope</li><li>Digitizing Stethoscope</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electronic stethoscope market is categorized into two main types: amplifying stethoscopes and digitizing stethoscopes. Amplifying stethoscopes enhance sound levels, allowing healthcare professionals to hear faint bodily sounds more clearly. They often include features like noise reduction to improve sound quality. Digitizing stethoscopes, on the other hand, convert acoustic sounds into digital signals for better analysis and visualization. These devices can record, store, and transmit data, facilitating remote consultations and advanced diagnostic capabilities, thus improving patient care.</p></p>
