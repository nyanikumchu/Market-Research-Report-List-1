<p><p>The hemp yarn market is divided into two primary types: natural hemp yarn and synthetic hemp yarn. Natural hemp yarn is made from pure hemp fibers, valued for its eco-friendliness, durability, and breathability, making it ideal for sustainable textiles. In contrast, synthetic hemp yarn blends hemp with man-made fibers, enhancing properties like elasticity and color retention, catering to a broader range of applications. Both types serve diverse industries, including fashion, home décor, and industrial textiles, reflecting varying consumer preferences and sustainability goals.</p></p>
