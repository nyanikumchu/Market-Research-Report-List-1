<p><p>The automated turf harvester market features two primary types: roll turf harvesters and slab turf harvesters. Roll turf harvesters are designed to efficiently cut and roll layers of sod into manageable rolls, making them ideal for large-scale installations. In contrast, slab turf harvesters specialize in cutting uniform turf slabs, which can be easier to handle in smaller projects or intricate layouts. Both types enhance efficiency and reduce labor costs, catering to the increasing demand for high-quality turf in landscaping and agriculture.</p></p>
