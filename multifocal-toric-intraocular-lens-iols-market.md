<p><p>The multifocal toric intraocular lenses (IOLs) market is divided into two key types: not pre-loaded IOLs and pre-loaded IOLs. Not pre-loaded IOLs require manual loading into the injector, offering flexibility in handling but needing more skill during implantation. Conversely, pre-loaded IOLs come ready-to-use in an injector, streamlining the surgical process and reducing the chances of contamination or handling errors. Both types cater to patients with astigmatism and presbyopia, enhancing vision quality post-cataract surgery.</p></p>
