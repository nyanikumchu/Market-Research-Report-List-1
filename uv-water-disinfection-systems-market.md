<p><p>UV water disinfection systems are categorized into medium pressure and low pressure types based on the type of UV lamp used and their operational applications. Medium pressure systems utilize higher intensity lamps that emit a broad spectrum of UV light, making them effective for treating large volumes of water quickly, commonly used in municipal applications. Low pressure systems, on the other hand, operate at lower intensity and efficiency, primarily effective for smaller-scale applications like residential or commercial water purification, providing reliable disinfection.</p></p>
