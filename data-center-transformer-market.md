<p><ul><li>Dry Type</li><li>Liquid Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Data Center Transformer market is primarily divided into two types: dry type and liquid type transformers. Dry type transformers use air as a cooling medium and are known for their safety, efficiency, and lower maintenance needs, making them suitable for environments sensitive to fire hazards. In contrast, liquid type transformers utilize insulating oil for cooling, offering greater efficiency for higher power applications but requiring more maintenance and careful handling of fluids. Both types cater to the diverse needs of data center infrastructures.</p></p>
