<p><p>The Analog-to-Digital Converter (ADC) market is segmented into various types based on their architecture and application. Pipeline ADCs offer high speed and resolution, making them suitable for video and communications. Successive Approximation Register (SAR) ADCs balance speed and power efficiency, often used in sensor applications. Sigma-Delta ADCs excel in high-precision measurements, ideal for audio and instrumentation. Flash ADCs provide the fastest conversion times, suitable for applications requiring rapid signal processing. Other ADC types include Delta-sigma and integrative converters, catering to diverse market needs.</p></p>
