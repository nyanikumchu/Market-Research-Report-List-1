<p><ul><li>Low Pass Filter</li><li>High Pass Filter</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>LC filters are essential components in various electronic applications, categorized into three main types: low pass filters (LPF), high pass filters (HPF), and other specialized filters. Low pass filters allow signals below a certain frequency to pass through while attenuating higher frequencies, making them ideal for audio applications. High pass filters, conversely, enable signals above a specific frequency to pass, often used in communication systems. Other filters, such as bandpass and band-stop filters, serve niche functions, catering to specific frequency ranges in diverse applications.</p></p>
