<p><p>Turf protection encompasses three market types: biological, chemical, and mechanical. Chemical protection relies on synthetic pesticides and fertilizers to control weeds, pests, and diseases effectively. Mechanical methods include physical techniques like mowing, aeration, and mulching to maintain turf quality.
