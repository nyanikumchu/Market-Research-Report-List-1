<p><p>The industrial filtration paper market is categorized primarily into three types: qualitative filtration papers, quantitative filtration papers, and others. Qualitative filtration papers are designed for general laboratory use, providing a basic separation of particulates without a specific measurement of retention. Quantitative filtration papers, on the other hand, are used for precise applications where exact measurements of particulates are required, ensuring high purity levels. The "others" category includes specialized papers tailored for specific industrial applications, offering tailored properties and performance characteristics.</p></p>
