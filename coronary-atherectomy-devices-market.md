<p><p>Coronary atherectomy devices are specialized tools used to treat obstructed coronary arteries. Laser atherectomy utilizes focused laser energy to vaporize plaque. Orbital atherectomy employs a diamond-coated crown that orbits around the artery, effectively removing plaque. Rotational atherectomy uses a high-speed rotating burr to grind away plaque. Other devices include directional and excimer atherectomy systems. Each type has unique mechanisms, advantages, and clinical applications, catering to varying patient needs in coronary artery disease management.</p></p>
