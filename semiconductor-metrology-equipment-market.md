<p><p>Semiconductor metrology equipment encompasses tools used to measure and characterize semiconductor materials and devices. The optical metrology segment utilizes light-based techniques, such as photometry and interferometry, to assess surface features and layer thicknesses, providing high-resolution measurements. In contrast, the electron beam metrology segment employs electron beams to achieve nanoscale precision, allowing for detailed analysis of material properties and defect detection. Both types are critical for ensuring the accuracy and quality of semiconductor manufacturing processes.</p></p>
