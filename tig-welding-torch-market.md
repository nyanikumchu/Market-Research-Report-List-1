<p><strong>The TIG Welding Torch Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Water Cooled</li><li>Air Cooled</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The TIG welding torch market is categorized into two main types: water-cooled and air-cooled torches. Water-cooled torches utilize a cooling system that circulates water through the torch body, allowing for extended use without overheating, making them ideal for high-duty applications. In contrast, air-cooled torches rely on ambient air for cooling, making them more portable and suitable for lighter use or less demanding tasks. Each type serves different welding environments and user needs, influencing market dynamics.</p></p>
