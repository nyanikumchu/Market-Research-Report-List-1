<p><p>The hydrophobic glass market features diverse technologies, primarily hydrophobic coating technology and nanofilm technology. Hydrophobic coating technology involves applying specialized chemical layers to glass surfaces, enhancing water repellency and reducing dirt adhesion. Nanofilm technology, on the other hand, utilizes ultra-thin films at the nanoscale to create a water-repellent effect while maintaining transparency and durability. Both technologies are increasingly adopted in various applications, including automotive, construction, and electronics, driven by demand for low-maintenance and self-cleaning surfaces.</p></p>
