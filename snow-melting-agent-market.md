<p><p>The snow melting agent market is divided into two main types: liquid and solid agents. Liquid snow melting agents are typically used for rapid snow and ice melting, providing immediate action and easier application through spraying. They often contain potassium or calcium-based compounds. Solid snow melting agents, on the other hand, come in granular or pellet form, requiring distribution over surfaces to gradually dissolve and melt snow. Both types serve to enhance safety and accessibility during winter conditions.</p></p>
