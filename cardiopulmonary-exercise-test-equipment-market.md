<p><p>The cardiopulmonary exercise test (CPET) equipment market comprises two primary types: treadmill-based and exercise bike-based systems. Treadmill CPET is widely used for assessing cardiorespiratory fitness, providing dynamic, full-body stress testing that mimics real-life activities. On the other hand, exercise bike CPET offers a controlled environment for more precise workload adjustments, benefiting patients with limited mobility or specific health conditions. Both types play a crucial role in diagnosing cardiovascular and pulmonary diseases, guiding rehabilitation programs, and evaluating fitness levels.</p></p>
