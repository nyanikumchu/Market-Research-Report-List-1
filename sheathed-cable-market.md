<p><p>The sheathed cable market is broadly categorized into nonmetallic-sheathed and metallic-sheathed cables. Nonmetallic-sheathed cables, often made of plastic materials like PVC, provide insulation and are lightweight, making them ideal for residential wiring and indoor applications. Conversely, metallic-sheathed cables utilize materials like aluminum or steel for the outer layer, offering enhanced durability and protection against physical damage, making them suitable for industrial and outdoor settings. Each type serves distinct purposes based on environment and safety requirements.</p></p>
