<p><p>On-The-Go Breakfast Cereals encompass two main market types: Ready-To-Eat (RTE) and Ready-To-Drink (RTD). RTD cereals are packaged in liquid form, such as milk-based or plant-based beverages infused with cereal ingredients, providing a portable and nutritious meal alternative. Both categories cater to busy lifestyles, offering convenience, nutrition, and taste for consumers seeking efficient breakfast solutions.</p></p>
