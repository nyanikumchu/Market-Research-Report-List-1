<p><strong>The Vintage Stereo Turntables Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct-drive Turntable</li><li>Belt-drive Turntable</li><li>Idler-wheel Turntable</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The vintage stereo turntable market comprises three main types: direct-drive, belt-drive, and idler-wheel turntables. Direct-drive models feature a motor directly coupled to the platter, offering quick start-up times and consistent speeds, favored by DJs. Belt-drive turntables use a rubber belt to isolate vibrations and provide smoother playback, appealing to audiophiles. Idler-wheel turntables employ a mechanical coupling system, known for their robust construction and warm sound, attracting collectors and enthusiasts. Each type caters to different needs and preferences in vinyl playback.</p></p>
