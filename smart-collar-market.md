<p><strong>The Smart Collar Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>GPS Based</li><li>Radio Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Smart Collar market primarily consists of GPS-based and radio-based technologies. GPS-based smart collars utilize satellite signals to track pets' locations in real-time, offering extensive range and accuracy for outdoor activities. In contrast, radio-based smart collars use short-range radio frequencies for tracking, ideal for close-proximity monitoring, such as training or in confined areas. Both types enhance pet safety and allow owners to monitor their pets' activities, but they cater to different tracking needs based on environment and distance.</p></p>
