<p><p>The amphoteric surfactants market comprises various types, including betaine, amine oxide, amphoacetate, amphopropionate, and sultaine. Amphoacetates are versatile and widely used in mild formulations, while amphopropionates are recognized for their high solubility and compatibility with various ingredients. Sultaines provide good foam and are often found in shampoos and cleansers. Together, these surfactants cater to diverse applications across industries.</p></p>
