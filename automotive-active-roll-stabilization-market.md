<p><p>The Automotive Active Roll Stabilization market comprises two primary types: rotary actuators and linear actuators. Rotary actuators use a rotational motion to adjust the suspension forces, enhancing vehicle stability during cornering. They are typically more compact and suitable for various automotive designs. In contrast, linear actuators operate through linear motion to control suspension dynamics, offering precise adjustments in response to road conditions. Both types contribute to improved handling, comfort, and safety, making them essential in modern vehicle dynamics systems.</p></p>
