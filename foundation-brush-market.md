<p><p>The foundation brush market comprises two primary types: animal hair and synthetic hair brushes. However, they can be less ethical and more expensive. Synthetic hair brushes, made from man-made fibers, offer enhanced durability, easy maintenance, and are often favored by vegan consumers. These brushes can also provide a diverse range of textures and finishes, catering to varying makeup preferences.</p></p>
