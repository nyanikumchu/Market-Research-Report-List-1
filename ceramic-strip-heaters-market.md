<p><p>The Ceramic Strip Heaters market consists of two main types: Radiant Ceramic Strip Heaters (PCTR) and Rigid Ceramic Strip Heaters (PCR). Radiant Ceramic Strip Heaters are designed to emit infrared radiation, providing efficient heat transfer for applications requiring immediate and focused heating. In contrast, Rigid Ceramic Strip Heaters offer structural stability, suitable for applications needing consistent temperatures over extended periods. Both types are utilized in various industries, including manufacturing, food processing, and HVAC systems, for their durability and high-temperature performance.</p></p>
