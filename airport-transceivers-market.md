<p><ul><li>UHF Airport Transceivers</li><li>VHF Airport Transceivers</li><li>HF Airport Transceivers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The airport transceivers market is categorized into three main types based on frequency: UHF, VHF, and HF transceivers. UHF airport transceivers operate in the ultra-high frequency range, ensuring reliable communication over shorter distances, essential for airport operations. VHF transceivers function in the very high frequency range, providing effective long-range communication for air traffic control and pilot interactions. HF transceivers, working in the high frequency range, enable communication over vast distances, making them suitable for international flight operations and remote areas.</p></p>
