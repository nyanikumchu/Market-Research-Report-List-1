<p><p>The ceramic bearings market is categorized into three main types: ZrO2 (zirconia), Si3N4 (silicon nitride), and other ceramics. ZrO2 bearings are known for their high toughness and corrosion resistance, making them suitable for applications in harsh environments. Si3N4 bearings offer superior wear resistance and lower friction, ideal for high-speed operations. Other types may include aluminum oxide and titanium nitride, each providing unique properties that cater to specialized applications across various industries, including aerospace, automotive, and medical devices.</p></p>
