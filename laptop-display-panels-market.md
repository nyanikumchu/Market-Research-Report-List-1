<p><p>The laptop display panel market primarily includes LCD (Liquid Crystal Display) and OLED (Organic Light Emitting Diode) technologies. LCD panels, widely used for their cost-effectiveness and energy efficiency, rely on backlighting and exhibit limited color range and contrast. OLED panels, although more expensive, offer superior color accuracy, deeper blacks, and faster response times due to self-emitting pixels. As demand for high-quality visuals and gaming experiences rises, the market sees a growing shift towards OLED technology, especially among premium laptops.</p></p>
