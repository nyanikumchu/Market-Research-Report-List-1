<p><ul><li>Short Persistence Phosphorescent Pigment</li><li>Long Persistence Phosphorescent Pigment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The phosphorescent pigments market is divided into two main types: short persistence and long persistence phosphorescent pigments. Short persistence pigments glow for a brief period, typically lasting from a few seconds to a couple of minutes after exposure to light, making them suitable for applications like safety signs and emergency lighting. In contrast, long persistence pigments emit light for several hours, often utilized in products such as glow-in-the-dark toys, wall paints, and decorative items, enhancing their visibility in dark environments.</p></p>
