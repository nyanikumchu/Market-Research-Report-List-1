<p><p>The benchtop plasma cleaner market includes two primary types: TEM (Transmission Electron Microscope) plasma cleaners and SEM (Scanning Electron Microscope) plasma cleaners. TEM plasma cleaners are designed to prepare samples for high-resolution imaging by removing contaminants at the atomic level, ensuring optimal sample visibility. In contrast, SEM plasma cleaners focus on surface cleaning, enhancing the quality of imaging by eliminating surface debris and oxidation. Both types are essential for maintaining sample integrity in electron microscopy applications, catering to research and industrial needs.</p></p>
