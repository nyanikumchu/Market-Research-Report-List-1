<p><p>The bridge sensor market comprises several configurations: Quarter-bridge, Half-bridge, Full-bridge, and Others. The Quarter-bridge utilizes one active strain gauge with three inactive gauges, ideal for low-cost applications. The Half-bridge configuration employs two active gauges, enhancing measurement accuracy and sensitivity. The Full-bridge setup features four active gauges, offering optimal precision for dynamic loads. Other types may include custom configurations or integrated sensors designed for specific applications. These configurations cater to diverse industrial needs, ensuring accurate stress and strain measurement.</p></p>
