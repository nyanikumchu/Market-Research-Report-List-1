<p><ul><li>Wet Drill Bits</li><li>Dry Drill Bits</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The construction industry core drill market is primarily segmented into wet and dry drill bits. Wet drill bits utilize water or other liquids to cool and lubricate the drilling process, reducing dust and heat while enhancing bit longevity. These are ideal for harder materials. Dry drill bits operate without water, relying on airflow for cooling, making them suitable for softer materials where dust control is less critical. Each type serves distinct applications, influencing performance, efficiency, and project outcomes in construction.</p></p>
